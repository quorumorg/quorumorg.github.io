Out Perform

Quorum advocates for increased representation of openly LGBT individuals on the boards of publicly traded corporations.

Greater Perspective Means
Smarter Decisions:

It is well documented that board diversity makes for better decision making, which ultimately leads to stronger governance and superior corporate performance.

In addition, we believe increased LGBT representation on boards creates corporate environments more open to diversity, which helps draw the greatest degree of talent to the workforce and supports increased diversity in senior management roles.

Our Mission

Our goal is to leverage the talent of experienced LGBT corporate executives to help board chairs and CEOs enhance board composition and corporate performance.

Quorum advocates for increased representation of openly LGBT individuals on the boards of publicly traded corporations.  We make the case that diversity enhances performance, and we help corporations take advantage of those benefits by providing qualified LGBT candidates who add value to corporate boards.

Know Great Talent?

Quorum is building a high-quality list of openly LGBT candidates who would make outstanding board candidates. When considering candidates for board seats, corporations typically consider the following criteria:

  • Relevant experience or expertise
  • Senior leadership or governance experience
  • Employment history
  • Past or current board posts
  • Availability

Quorum

The minimum number of members of an assembly or society that must be present at any of its meetings to make the proceedings of that meeting valid.

We named ourselves Quorum because the quality of our decisions improves as the diversity of the group making the decisions increases. Every company should ask themselves: "Do we have a Quorum sufficient to make the best possible decisions?"
